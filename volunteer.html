Volunteer & Aid


Are you passionate about elephant conservation and looking for a way to get involved more directly either with or without a background in wildlife? Can you apply your skills to help further our mission? Contact us with your own suggestions.


VOLUNTEER


We are seeking up to 2 volunteers per month to help feed and bathe baby elephants. No prior experience or formal training in biology is required, but preference will be given to those who are demonstrably interested in a conservation career.

Volunteer is to be based in Sri Lanka and must be able to cover the cost of their own travel, food and accommodation, be self-sufficient and experienced with travel and cultures in Asia. The fieldstation is basic and located in a rural area with electricity but limited internet.
