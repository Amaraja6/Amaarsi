Adopt an Elephant

Adopt an elephant

Each animal is a REAL individual, named according to the letter of the alphabet corresponding to its family as part of the long-term study of wild Asian elephants supported by Amaarsi Elephant Tranist Home. By adopting an elephant you can help us continue to fund this unique research project, and our community-based elephant conservation initiatives.

Likki

Likki is the youngest member of the L family, born in 2018.
The L's are a well-known and beloved family that's been studied for over 14 years! Adopt Likki to learn their stories.
